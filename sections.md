# Applicant mode headers:

- General comparison for each section in his cv named after the section. Job decsription vs current cv. This should include missing tools, missing skills, missing experience, missing education, ..etc. (Separate header for each section)

- General improvement suggestions: Anything that can help the applicant to improve his CV for that position.


# Company mode headers:

- Ranking & Match Overview: (Overall score and a brief summary of why this candidate is a good fit for the position)

- Why this candidate fits the position.

- Things to keep in mind (unclear points in the CV but required in the job description)

- Questions to ask the candidate based on his CV (both technical and HR related)
